# **App Name**: GTTC Hub

## Core Features:

- Course Catalog: Display available courses with details such as duration, fees, and syllabus.
- Online Application Form: Enable students to fill and submit admission forms online.
- Teacher Directory: Showcase teachers with their profiles and expertise.
- FAQ Generator: AI Tool that will answer frequently asked questions regarding the training center and its services.
- Contact Information: Display contact details, including phone numbers, email, and address.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Sky blue (#0097E6) for a vibrant and trustworthy feel, inspired by the training center's poster.
- Background color: Light blue (#F0F8FF), very slightly bluer and desaturated compared to the primary, to create a clean and calm atmosphere.
- Accent color: Blue-green (#00A8FF), a vibrant analogous color to the primary, for interactive elements and highlights.
- Body and headline font: 'Hind Siliguri' (sans-serif) for readability and consistency with the training center's branding.
- Font Awesome icons to represent different sections and actions, enhancing usability.
- Use a grid-based layout to organize content effectively and maintain responsiveness.
- Subtle transitions and hover effects to improve user experience.
